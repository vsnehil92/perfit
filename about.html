Who We Are · Since 1990

Manufactured & Marketed by Ankur Plastic Industries

Established in the year 1990 in Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, Perfit Pipes and Fittings is a trusted brand manufactured by Ankur Plastic Industries. We are engaged in the manufacturing of high-quality PVC Pipes and PVC Fittings for a wide range of plumbing, agricultural, and industrial applications.

Our products are widely appreciated for their superior quality, durability, dimensional accuracy, and competitive pricing. With a strong commitment to customer satisfaction, quality manufacturing, and timely delivery, we have built a trusted reputation and a loyal customer base across the market.

Our Foundations

Mission, Vision & Quality Commitment

Our Mission

To manufacture dependable, leakproof PVC pipes and fittings that are accessible and affordable for every plumbing, agricultural and industrial project.

Our Vision

To be a trusted, recognisable name in pipes & fittings across Uttar Pradesh and beyond, built on over three decades of consistent quality and customer trust.
