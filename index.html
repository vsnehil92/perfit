About Perfit · Since 1990

A Name You Can Fit Your Trust In

Established in 1990 in Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, Perfit Pipes and Fittings is a trusted brand manufactured by Ankur Plastic Industries. We manufacture high-quality PVC pipes and PVC fittings for a wide range of plumbing, agricultural and industrial applications.

  • Manufacturing PVC pipes & fittings since 1990
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing
  • Superior quality, durability & dimensional accuracy
  • Competitive pricing & timely delivery
